<p>His research focuses on whole-engine aerodynamic stability assessment and reduced-order modeling of surge dynamics in aero-engines. He develops system-level rapid prediction theories and models for surge processes, quantifying the effects of different surge-inducing methods and control parameters to support surge-control logic design and whole-engine surge-test planning.</p>
